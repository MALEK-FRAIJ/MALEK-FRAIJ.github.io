Sell your home with confidence

Your home is more than just a place. It is a reflection of your unique personality and style. It is a story. Let us help you write the next chapter by providing the tools and resources you need to successfully sell your home. We help you showcase its best features and attract the right buyers to ensure a successful sale and a happy ending to your home ownership journey. A new chapter in your life.

Sell your home yourself

Deciding to sell your home yourself is referred to as for-sale-by-owner (FSBO). The FSBO process is similar to traditional selling, but without the help of a real estate agent. In this case, you’re responsible for the home prep, marketing, showings, and negotiations.

When selling yourself, start with home prep, staging,and hiring a professional photographer.Once your marketing materials are ready, research comparable homes to help price your home.

Why Sell FSBO

  • Avoid paying a listing agent commission

  • Advertise your listing for free on Sakan-Plus

  • Flexibility and control from start to finish

How to sell FSBO

When selling yourself, start with home prep, staging,and hiring a professional photographer.Once your marketing materials are ready, research comparable homes to help price your home. Then,create a listing on Sakan-Plus. You’ll likely host home showings or open houses. Then select an offer, negotiate,accept and close.
